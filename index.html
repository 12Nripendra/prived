Everything You Need to Know

Our Services, Explained

From molecule to market — here's a detailed look at how we partner with pharmaceutical innovators at every stage.

Drug discovery is the process of identifying and validating new molecular entities that could become medicines. At PRIVED Life, we combine AI-driven target identification, high-throughput screening of our 2M+ compound library, and state-of-the-art structural biology to rapidly progress from target to lead candidate. Our medicinal chemistry teams specialize in optimizing drug-like properties including ADMET profiles, selectivity, and metabolic stability.
Target ID HTS Screening Lead Optimization ADMET Profiling
Our API manufacturing capabilities span from gram-scale for early clinical supply to multi-metric-ton commercial production. We operate 8 dedicated synthesis suites with advanced flow chemistry, biocatalysis, and photochemistry capabilities. All API manufacturing is conducted under cGMP guidelines with full ICH Q7 compliance. We support controlled substances (DEA Schedule I–V) with dedicated secure facilities.
GMP API cGMP Synthesis Controlled Substances Scale-up
From preformulation to commercial-scale manufacturing, our drug product team handles oral solids, semi-solids, sterile injectables, and complex dosage forms. We specialize in challenging formulations including poorly soluble compounds (using hot-melt extrusion, spray drying), modified-release systems, and highly potent formulations. Our packaging line supports blister, bottle, and unit-dose configurations.
Oral Solids Injectables Modified Release HPAPI Handling
ADCs represent one of the most exciting frontiers in oncology. PRIVED Life offers end-to-end ADC development — from linker-payload synthesis and DAR (drug-to-antibody ratio) optimization to full GMP clinical manufacturing. Our dedicated ADC suites feature containment systems for cytotoxic payloads and comprehensive analytical capabilities including HIC, SEC, and mass spectrometry characterization.
Linker Synthesis Bioconjugation Cytotoxic Payload Analytical Char.
Navigating global regulatory requirements demands specialized expertise. Our regulatory affairs team has successfully completed 150+ filings with FDA, EMA, PMDA, and Health Canada. We provide comprehensive CMC support including Drug Master File (DMF/ASMF) preparation, IND/CTA/NDA authoring, and post-approval change management. We maintain a 95% first-cycle approval rate.
FDA Filings EMA Submissions DMF/ASMF CMC Strategy
Our quality management system is built on ICH Q10 principles with a culture of continuous improvement. We offer stand-alone analytical services including method development and validation (ICH Q2), forced degradation studies, genotoxic impurity analysis (ICH M7), and long-term stability programs under ICH Q1 conditions. Our labs are fully equipped with HPLC, GC, LC-MS/MS, NMR, and ICP-MS instruments.
ICH Q10 QMS Method Validation Stability Studies Impurity Profiling
Choosing the right CDMO partner is a critical decision. PRIVED Life International differentiates itself through truly integrated capabilities (no handoffs to third parties), speed to IND in as little as 12 months for novel molecules, transparent project management with dedicated program managers, and competitive pricing backed by our India-based manufacturing infrastructure. Our client retention rate stands at 94% over the last decade.
Integrated CDMO Speed to IND Cost-Effective 94% Retention
